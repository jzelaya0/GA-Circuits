HOW ADULTS LEARN:

PRINCIPLES 1 - 3

Before we begin, let’s remind ourselves of the learning objectives for this activity:

  1. Describe the main principles of adult learning theory

  2. Identify how to use the principles of adult learning theory to make your instruction more effective

Knowing how adults learn helps us understand our students and how to create effective, high-impact learning experiences for them.

We utilize a set of six adult learning principles to guide our approach to instruction at GA. These principles structure everything we do, from lesson design, to instructor training, to building better products.

Learning experiences for adults need to include

  1. Opportunites for self direction
  2. Opportunites to draw on life experiences
  3. Relevance to learner
  4. Problem-centered learning objectives
  5. Social elements
  6. Active learning

Principles of Adult Learning Theory

The principles of adult learning theory, or andragogy, prescribe the types of learning activities and environments that maximize adults’ learning potential.
