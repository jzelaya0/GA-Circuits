GA Education Model

GA has taken the best parts of traditional education and the best parts of agile product development and created a model to help students gain practice and mastery of 21st century skills.

We champion

  • Activity-driven instruction & active learning

  • Application and practice-based learning design

  • Maximizing student’s value
